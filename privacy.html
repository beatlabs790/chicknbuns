Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 26, 2026 Security Status: Verified

1. Introduction

Welcome to CHICK'N'BUN'S Privacy Policy page. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any details you share with us. This policy describes how we handle information when you browse our premium riverside website and use our cart services.

2. Information Collection

Our website is designed as a lightweight, pre-rendered static experience that collects very minimal data to run correctly. We do not use third-party backend servers to track your browsing habits:

  • Order Details: Any name, item selection, or instruction you provide in the Cart page is compiled solely in your browser to prepare WhatsApp order text messages.
  • Local Storage: We store your active shopping cart items and light/dark mode selection in your browser's local storage so that your configurations persist when you refresh or navigate pages.

3. Cookies and Consent

We use essential cookies and local storage tokens to store system preferences (such as your theme style). We do not run advertisement tracking pixels. By selecting "Accept Cookies" on our banner, you consent to our use of these essential local storage elements to guarantee zero lag during navigation.

4. Data Storage and Transfer

All items placed in your cart remain stored entirely client-side. When you choose to place an order, the details are transferred directly into WhatsApp (managed by Meta Technologies) or transmitted verbally when you execute a direct call to our phone number (+91 9801351304).

5. Updates to This Policy

We may modify our site configurations from time to time. We encourage visitors to review this policy page occasionally to stay informed about how we maintain clean, secure user experiences.
