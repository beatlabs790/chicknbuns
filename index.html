Our Story

Crafting Premium Riverside Vibe Since Day One

Nestled along the serene banks of the Ganga River at Kangan Ghat, CHICK'N'BUN'S was born out of a simple passion: to serve premium, mouth-watering street food with an unmatchable atmosphere.

From our thematic Peaky Blinders corner to our glowing neon signage, every square inch of our lounge is built to offer a cozy, aesthetic hangout spot for friends, families, and food lovers alike. We believe that great service, humble smiles, and fresh quality ingredients are what make an experience truly unforgettable.

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

Are Veg and Non-Veg items prepared separately?
Yes, absolutely. We maintain strict hygiene standards and cook all vegetarian items in separate utensils and cooking zones to respect our customers' dietary preferences.
Do you host private events, birthdays or reunions?
Yes, we do! Our cafe interior features an aesthetic Peaky Blinders corner and neon signs, which is a perfect setting for birthday celebrations, cozy reunions, and gatherings. You can book a spot using the table reservation form above.
Do you offer home delivery in Patna City?
We primarily offer self-pickup and takeaways. However, for delivery inside Patna City (near Kangan Ghat and Chowk area), please contact us directly at +91 9801351304 to confirm if delivery staff is currently available.
What are your cafe operating timings?
CHICK'N'BUN'S is open every day of the week from 11:00 AM to 10:00 PM. We look forward to serving you!
